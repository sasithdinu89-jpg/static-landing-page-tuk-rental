Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about self-driving in Sri Lanka.

Yes, tourists can rent and drive a Tuk Tuk in Sri Lanka. However, your International Driving Permit (IDP) alone is not sufficient. You are required to have a specific recognition permit from the Automobile Association of Ceylon (AAC). SK Tuk Rides handles this entire paperwork process for you before you arrive.

Driving a Tuk Tuk has a learning curve due to the handlebar gear shifting mechanism. However, most people learn quickly. We provide a comprehensive 1 to 2-hour driving lesson with every rental to ensure you are confident and safe before hitting the road.

Yes, all our Tuk Tuk rentals come with comprehensive rental insurance that covers you and the vehicle. We want you to explore Sri Lanka with total peace of mind.

Our rental rates start from $11 USD per day for long-term rentals. The standard 'Explorer' model is $16/day and the premium 'Pro' model with Bluetooth speakers and extra comfort is $18/day.
